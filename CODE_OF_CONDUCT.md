# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want AgentSec Preflight discussions to be practical, respectful, and useful to people building or reviewing AI-agent toolchains.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent while still being precise about risks
- avoid harassment, intimidation, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- keep sensitive data, credentials, and exploit details out of public threads
- respect project claim boundaries and avoid unsupported certification, readiness, endorsement, or authorization claims
